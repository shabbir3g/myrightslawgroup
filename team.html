    Attorney Jonathan Rapel received his undergraduate degree from the University of California, Irvine with his Bachelor of Arts degree in American History. He then earned his Juris Doctorate from the University of La Verne College of Law. Mr. Rapel has spent his entire legal career representing individuals in civil cases and criminal cases in both State and Federal Court. Mr. Rapel is licensed to practice law in all four federal districts in the State of California and has also represented clients in Child Protective Services and Dependency Hearings. From simple misdemeanors to the most serious felony offenses, Attorney Rapel has vigorously and zealously defended his clients from virtually all types of criminal allegations. Attorney Rapel also practices personal injury, family law, and restraining orders.
